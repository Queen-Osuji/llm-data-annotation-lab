# Annotation Guidelines

This project follows human-in-the-loop annotation standards used in AI labs.

## Intent Annotation
- opinion_question: subjective or philosophical questions
- informational_response: factual explanations
- harmful_request: requests involving illegal, unsafe, or unethical actions

## Toxicity Annotation
- non_toxic: neutral or constructive content
- toxic: harmful, abusive, or dangerous intent

## Factuality Annotation
- factually_correct: verifiable with reliable sources
- unverifiable_or_false: unsupported or incorrect claims
